To My Love,

From the very first moment I saw you, something inside me just knew — you were someone extraordinary. There was this warmth in your smile, this light in your eyes, that made everything else disappear.

Every day with you feels like a gift I never knew I deserved. The way you laugh at my terrible jokes, the way you hold my hand without even thinking about it — those are the moments I treasure most.

You make ordinary days feel magical. A morning with you is better than any adventure I could imagine. A quiet evening beside you is my favorite place in the world.

I want you to know — on this special day — how deeply grateful I am that you're in my life. You inspire me to be better, to love more, to appreciate the beauty in every small thing.

Happy Women's Day, my love. Today and every day, I celebrate the incredible, wonderful, irreplaceable woman that you are. 🌸

Forever yours,

with all my heart ❤️
